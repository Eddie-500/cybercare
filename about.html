Our Story

CyberCare was born from frontline responders who saw a better path

WWRO volunteers witnessed the impact cyber attacks have on the most vulnerable institutions. CyberCare bridges the gap between enterprise-grade protection and the realities of schools, NGOs, and emergency services.

Mission

Empower communities to operate fearlessly online by combining threat intelligence, preparedness, and accessible education.

Vision

A world where digital-first responders can coordinate effortlessly and every organization has a cyber resilience plan.

Values

Human-first support, open knowledge sharing, and a relentless commitment to equitable access to security resources.

Impact timeline

A growing movement grounded in global collaboration

2019

First responders unite

WWRO volunteers coordinate their first cross-border incident response, revealing the need for a shared digital playbook.

2021

CyberCare launches

The team ships the inaugural CyberCare platform with multilingual training modules for educators and local governments.

2024

Global network activated

40+ partner organizations rely on CyberCare for preparedness drills, live monitoring, and shared intelligence briefings.
